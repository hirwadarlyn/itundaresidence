Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about living at Itunda Residence.

What is included in the rent?

Rent includes free breakfast, laundry facilities, WiFi, high-speed fiber internet, 24/7 security, access to all amenities maintenance services, and basic utilities. Additional utilities are billed separately based on usage.

Are pets allowed in the residence?

No, pets are not allowed in the residence.

What parking options are available?

Each room comes with access to shared parking. Additional parking spaces and visitor parking are available for an extra fee. Valet parking service is complimentary for residents.

Explore Kigali Tours And Other Different Places

Discover the vibrant attractions and cultural landmarks near Itunda Residence.

Kigali Convention Centre

Iconic landmark hosting international conferences, events, and offering panoramic city views from its dome-shaped structure.

8 minutes drive

BK Arena

Rwanda's largest indoor arena hosting major sporting events, concerts, and international conferences with world-class facilities.

12 minutes drive

Nyandungu Eco Park

A serene urban wetland park perfect for nature walks, bird watching, and enjoying Kigali's beautiful natural environment.

15 minutes drive

Kigali Genocide Memorial (Urwibutso)

A poignant memorial site honoring the victims of the 1994 genocide, offering educational exhibits and peaceful gardens for reflection.

10 minutes drive

Nyungwe National Park

One of Africa's oldest rainforests, home to chimpanzees, colobus monkeys, and over 300 bird species with breathtaking canopy walks.

5-6 hours drive

Akagera National Park

Rwanda's only savannah park with Big Five animals including lions, elephants, rhinos, and diverse wildlife in beautiful landscapes.

2-3 hours drive

Lake Kivu

One of Africa's Great Lakes, offering beautiful beaches, water activities, and stunning sunset views along Rwanda's western border.

3-4 hours drive

Kigali City Tour

Explore Rwanda's vibrant capital with its clean streets, local markets, art galleries, restaurants, and cultural experiences.

Within city

Volcanoes National Park

Home to mountain gorillas and golden monkeys, offering trekking adventures in Rwanda's volcanic landscape.

2-3 hours drive
